About

Kai
Bubar

Web Designer

I've been designing for the web for over six years, working with everyone from independent photographers to small product companies who need a site that actually reflects the quality of what they do.

My process is collaborative by nature. I ask a lot of questions upfront so I understand not just what you need, but why you need it — and who it's for. That research shapes every decision, from layout to color to copy hierarchy.

When I'm not designing, I'm probably riding my bike, reading about typographic history, or finding yet another interesting way to make CSS do something it probably wasn't meant to do.

I take on a small number of projects at a time to make sure every client gets the attention they deserve. If you've got something in mind, reach out early — availability fills up fast.
